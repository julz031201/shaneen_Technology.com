Technology

Technology affects almost every aspect of 21st century life, from transport efficiency and safety, to access to food and healthcare, socialization and productivity. The power of the internet has enabled global communities to form an ideas and resources to be shared more easily. Modern technology has paved the way for multi-functional devices like the smartwatch and the smartphone. Computers are increasingly faster, more portable, and higher-powered than ever before. With all of these revolutions, technology has also made our lives easier, faster, better, and more fun.

ICT a Great Contributor

ICT is a power tool when it comes to innovation. When you study ICT, you can handle things that are related to technology such as computers, mobile phones and others. If you wanted to contribute great in a society using ICT, you do the following: Use your technology to search and have access for information that can be useful and helpful to the society. Such information includes latest updates on current issues that affecting almost the whole city or country. Be aware of the source of your information and share it responsibly. Be a responsible social media user. You can contribute a lot if you only share true and legit information and you don't support online bullying, identity theft and other activities that could harm others like participating in hacking and cyberterrorism. Use ICT to teach others. Use ICT for economic growth. Support local online businesses. Use ICT to connect people by engaging in seminars and other learning meetings.

Access Information as a Student

As a student and amidst of the current situation, the use of ICT is very in-demand nowadays. Social distancing and restrictions in public places like schools, malls etc., is being exercised by the proper authorities. The main reason for this is the current condition of our world, the rapid growing of infected people with Covid-19 cases, a deadly airborne virus which causes death to hundred thousand of people worldwide. Since there are restrictions in the streets and public places, the use of ICT is being a necessity. People are becoming inclined in using different communication and IT devices and technologies to continue their work, studies and daily tasks. This includes me as a student. Instead of going to school everyday, I get to use my computer and logged into the internet and school portal to access my lessons. I cannot visit my friends often, therefore, I have to communicate with them via online platforms. Since more people are using the ICT the information in the internet is becoming wider is scope and numbers. Not all information posted online is reliable and true. I have to be more cautious and careful in getting information. I need to verify it first from the source before I could use it.
